Undocumented behaviour

One of the functionality that we needed in Calabash was the ability to pass data to the app being automated at runtime. This is so we can change certain functionality in the app for a particular test, such as setting a staging environment URL. While this functionality is already there for iOS, it was sorely missing from the Android side of Calabash. After talking to one of our Android developers, Akshay, I discovered that Android could take in such data using the Intents system, and I set out to find a way to inject them to Calabash‚Äôs launch Intent. After poring through the code, and even forking the Calabash-Android repository once, I discovered that the groundwork was already laid for this functionality, but it was not documented in any form anywhere.
